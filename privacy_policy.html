Privacy Policy - Faceless Automation

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Data Collection

Faceless Automation collects only the minimum data necessary to operate:

  • TikTok API credentials (securely stored)
  • Video metadata (title, description)
  • Posted content timestamps

2. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to post content to TikTok on your behalf. We do not:

  • Share data with third parties
  • Use data for marketing
  • Store content longer than necessary

3. Security

We implement standard security practices to protect your data and API credentials.

4. Your Rights

You can request data deletion at any time. Deleting your account removes all stored credentials.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes take effect immediately upon posting.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact the app administrator.

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the app administrator.
